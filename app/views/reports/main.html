Reports

  • Transaction History Account Balance with summary of recent and pending transactions.
  • Timelogs A summary of all the payments and work that took place during the week.
  • Weekly Agency Timesheet by Freelancer Reports on time and earnings by your freelancers during a given week.
  • Weekly Agency Timesheet by Client Reports on time and earnings from clients during a given week.
  • Agency Timesheet Details Displays a detailed report of work done by a freelancer for a given client during a selected time period.
  • Time Analyze A configurable report of hours or charges during a selected time period.
  • Lifetime Billings by Client Shows the total amount billed with each of your clients.
  • Connects History Shows how many Connects you've used or purchased during a selected period of time.
